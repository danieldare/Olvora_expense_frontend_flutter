Trips help you group related expenses together, like expenses from a vacation, business trip, or group outing. Here's how to use them:

   - Tap **"Create Trip"** to group the expense
   - OR tap **"Skip"** to create the expense without a trip

## ğŸ‘¥ Sharing a Trip

### To Share a Trip:

1. Open **Trip Details** screen
2. Tap **"Share Trip"** button (only for private trips)
3. Enter the **User ID** of the person you want to invite
4. Tap **"Invite Participant"**
5. The trip becomes **SHARED** and the person is added

### What Happens When Shared:

- Other participants can see the trip
- You can split expenses among participants
- Participants can add messages
- All participants can view expenses

## ğŸ’° Splitting Expenses (Shared Trips Only)

### To Split an Expense:

1. Open **Trip Details** screen
2. Find the expense in the list
3. Tap the **"Split"** button next to the expense amount
4. In the split modal:
   - **If no split exists**: Tap **"Split Equally"** to divide among all participants
   - **If split exists**: View how it's divided, or delete it

### Split Types:

- **Equal Split**: Automatically divides expense equally among all participants
- **Exact Amounts**: (Future feature) Specify exact amounts per person
- **Percentage**: (Future feature) Split by percentage

## ğŸ Ending a Trip

### To Close a Trip:

1. From **Home Screen**: Tap **"End Trip"** in the Active Trip Banner
2. From **Trip Details**: Tap **"End Trip"** button
3. Confirm the action in the dialog
4. The trip becomes **CLOSED** and:
   - No new expenses can be added
   - A summary is generated
   - Trip moves to "Past Trips"

### What Happens:

- Trip status changes to **CLOSED**
- End time is set to now
- A read-only summary is created
- Trip disappears from Active Trip Banner
- Trip appears in "Past Trips" list

## â“ Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the Active Trip Banner"

- **Check**: Do you have an active trip?
- **Solution**: Create a trip first, or check if your trip was closed

### "I can't add expenses to my trip"

- **Check**: Is the trip still ACTIVE?
- **Solution**: Only active trips can have expenses added. Closed trips are read-only.

### "I can't split expenses"

- **Check**: Is the trip SHARED?
- **Solution**: Expense splitting is only available for shared trips. Share the trip first.

### "I can't share my trip"

- **Check**: Are you the trip owner?
- **Solution**: Only the trip owner can share trips and invite participants.
