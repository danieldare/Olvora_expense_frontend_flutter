## âœ¨ What You Can Do with Trips

### **View Trip Details**:
- Tap **"View Trip"** from banner
- OR tap trip card in Trips List
- See: Expenses, Participants, Messages, Actions

### **Share a Trip**:
1. Open Trip Details
2. Tap **"Share Trip"** button
3. Enter User ID of person to invite
4. Tap **"Invite Participant"**

### **Split Expenses** (Shared Trips Only):
1. Open Trip Details
2. Find expense in list
3. Tap **"Split"** button next to amount
4. Tap **"Split Equally"** to divide among participants

### **Add Messages** (Shared Trips Only):
1. Open Trip Details
2. Scroll to **"Messages"** section
3. Tap **"Add Message"**
4. Type message and save

### **End a Trip**:
1. From Home: Tap **"End Trip"** in banner
2. From Trip Details: Tap **"End Trip"** button
3. Confirm in dialog
4. Trip becomes read-only summary
